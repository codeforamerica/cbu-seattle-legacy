Dear Change by Us users,

Following the beta test, Seattle city staff have decided not to implement Change by Us this year and instead take a step back to do some assessment of engagement initiatives. We will be shutting off the site this Friday, Nov. 18, 2011.

The Code for America projects that will continue in Seattle include CityGroups -- citygroups.org -- and the Blockwatch Group Directory -- citygroups.org/blockwatch -- with support from the Seattle Police Department.

If you have anymore questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to Sol Villareal, the director of engagement for the office of the mayor, at sol.villareal@seattle.gov, or, to learn more about CityGroups and the Blockwatch Group Directory, contact Chacha Sikes at chacha@codeforamerica.org.

Thanks for your support this year,
2011 Code for America Fellows
